# different

2021-Aug-19
eng

-

I am a picture but try telling a story
You are a story but try painting an album
We are living in the same world
But seem to be from two different one
Is it funny, isn’t it?
Just like
small lizard in the rain
oh how did he get there
with the house he’s been crawling on
cannot seem to find a home
with all the food he’s been stepping on
still have to eat his own tail

“mọi chuyện dường như quá đỗi bình thường”
