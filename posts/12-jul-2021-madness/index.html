I.
They have four horsemen of apocalypse
He has four walls of contemplation
3 days ago, he named this wall Dabria
2 days ago, he named this Aeron
Yesterday, he named this one Brona
If it wasn’t for you, he would’ve named the last one Sterben
If it wasn’t for you, he would’ve named the last one Tom
If it wasn’t for you, he would descended to madness
If it wasn’t for you, he would pulled the trigger
But madness has finally come
And the bullet were fired
Effortlessly meaningless endeavor
Now he is talking to them

…
I don’t know

II.
yes, indeed

wall’s become mirror

talking back and waiting
but understand something, that
human are too afraid of the word

like a stone, he waited
till the day was gone
till the foggy head, faded
and the maze, he’ll go beyond
