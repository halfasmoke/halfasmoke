# mirror

2021-Aug-12
eng

-

a child stops smiling
a woman goes silence
a man never came back
the wind, raging, yelling
the cloud, pouring down the soil
the moon, turned dark
teardrops, swallowed the sun
the bird, now swimming
fingers, turned into matches
water, turned into oxygen
and ignited its very own soul
but there’s still another side of the mirror
and the sky is pretty much blue over here…

“mọi chuyện dường như quá đỗi bình thường”
-R
