# away it goes

2021-Aug-03
1 minute read
eng

-

There it goes, away it goes
The day goes by and something talking with nothing
Maybe expecting plays out too much
Maybe hope is persevering
A little jazz playing there
A little blue in this bottle
A little fish swimming slowly
The old train forgot direction to the castle
Angel wings are dropping, so seemingly
‘Chet Baker - Almost blue’
Wind sticks to the wall like glue
‘In the mood for love - Shigeru Umebayashi’
Moon howling at the wolf feeling so free
Maybe it was a dream, and I just woke up
It all turns upside down, I suppose
There it goes, away it goes…
