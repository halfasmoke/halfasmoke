# story and picture

2021-Aug-06
0 minute read
eng

-

telling a story is like painting a picture.
starting with a simple point, then a line, sometimes a curve, adding colour, favorite colour, good one, bad one just to make it diverse.
some may call it good, some may add a few details, and some may even tear the whole canvas apart.
but it doesn’t matter because paint can be drawn again, all the line, the curve, the detail are already there, and the original exists nowhere else but within the painter.
maybe next time will be prettier than the last…

-R
