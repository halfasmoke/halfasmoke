# a quiet night

2021-Jul-29
1 minute read
eng

-

it’s quiet the night
i can hear the cricket
it’s quiet the night
i can hear raindrop in my head
it’s quiet the night
i can hear my skin shedding
it’s quiet the night
i can hear my bone cracking
it’s quiet the night
i can hear my heartbeat
it’s quiet the night
i can hear footsteps out in the street
it’s quiet the night
i can hear the walls again
it’s quiet the night
i can hear the madness descend
it’s quiet the night
i can hear my soul screams in rejoice
it’s quiet the night
but i can’t seem to hear your voice

-R
