# anew

2021-Aug-11
eng

-

“If only you could answer,
If only your heart had courage to lift itself up and take a chance.
The promise of luminous eyes,
Beyond the dark curtain of my hollow soul…”

why does oneself can see such a beautiful thing?
maybe because now, in one’s mind, something has opened, and something has awaken…
or it’s already there to be read.

the bridge has once again, presented
hopefully, it won’t blow up halfway through…

“mọi chuyện dường như quá đỗi bình thường”
-R
