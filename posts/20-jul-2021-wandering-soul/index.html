# wandering soul

2021-Jul-20
eng

-

In endless realm of nothingness, obscure darkness
Exists something ethereal, unfathomable yet seemingly wondrous
Soul

Floating mindlessly, and…
One found another amongst the many
Stumbled upon each others
Somehow, in some way miraculously and strangely
Regardless

Let those unify
Resonate in frequency, echo through eternity
Then let those wander in the midst of stars
Delicately
Circling around the sun, dallying
Delight in the warmth, the golden gleam
Tastefulness in all between
Freely and unwillingly
Quietly and vividly

At last…
Without hurrying, fading away
Vow at the final embrace
Back to its realm, back to nothingness
Allow others to have
Space, time, fortunes..

“mọi chuyện dường như quá đỗi bình thường”
-R
