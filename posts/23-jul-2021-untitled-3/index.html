# untitled no.3

2021-Jul-23
0 minute read
eng

-

far too complex to describe
far too difficult to say out loud.
a man smoking cigarette at midnight,
a woman wishing by the lake,
a child went silent.
art using it’s abstract strokes,
a song blending its note variation.
an old couple sitting on park bench,
a young couple holding hands,
and a person hanging themselves.
try, they all did
as if giving all of those a shape that words could not
as if they might discover a clue that would lead to decency
…sometimes just a little too much

-R
