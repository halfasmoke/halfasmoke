# an answer ?

2021-Jul-03
1 minute read
eng

-

Going left is only right in some cases and going right is only right in some cases. Whether to choose to go left or right solely depends on the occasion and circumstances.
What if one chooses to stay in the middle and wait for the situation to act, to choose which path, left or right? But choosing to stay is also choosing to go either left or right, in a bigger picture, thus creating infinite paths, infinite choices in one’s mind.
So one finds oneself frozen in a midst of choices but why does one freeze? Is it because one is afraid of what path one takes is right or wrong?
There is no right or wrong, it’s just one’s choice or others, that is, mine or yours and there are countless ideas, reasons, even “wisdom” to justify any way of choosing.
Is that it? The final answer, but this answer is only a means, rarely an end and perhaps… one does not need answers, nor certainty, nor solace of this form.

-R
