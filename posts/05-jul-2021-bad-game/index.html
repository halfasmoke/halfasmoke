# bad Game

2021-Jul-05
eng

-

Link the fire to extend the age of Light or let it fade to introduce the age of men, dark. But in the age of dark, embers will dance, and once again, fire will be rekindled.
But whether to do or not to do, a loop is always created.
A circle that has no end nor beginning, a mere absurdity will bring every action to a halt.
There is no right answer nor there is wrong, it just is.
A way? The correct way or the wrong way, it’s matter not… It’s just mine or yours.
And there is something worth keeping, and worth telling; story.
Stories of each path one takes and the legend present in it.
And there is something worth remembering whether it’s a mere token or a symbol representing somebody or even a gravestone…
