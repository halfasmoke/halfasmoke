# a moment of freedom

2021-Jul-11
eng

-

a man with a boat in the middle of the lake is an act of freedom
slowly rowing his boat aimlessly in the midst of waves,
not necessarily knowing where to but in the man’s mind,
he knows exactly where the destination is,
solely because it is his boat.
not necessarily requiring companion or friend,
he knows exactly how to keep him sane,
solely because he himself, is at peace,
and at that moment, the lake

is also his…

“mọi chuyện dường như quá đỗi bình thường”
-R
