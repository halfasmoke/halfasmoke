# the barmaid and the ashen one

2021-May-26
2 minute read
eng

-

barmaid: “Last day, huh?”
ashen one: “I guess so”
barmaid: “Have something to say, don’t you?”
ashen one: “ *sigh * I think I dug myself a hole so deep that now I am lost under the ground, too far down from the surface, worse than that, I’ve been there for too long and the comfort is growing within myself. I no longer long for a way out but at the same time I can sense a tiny fracture of myself screaming, stretching to the sky but all of that, all the effort were in vain and so close to the edge of hopelessness. Maybe that is what’s left of my humanity…
*chuckle *
What if all of my humanity has finally lost?”
