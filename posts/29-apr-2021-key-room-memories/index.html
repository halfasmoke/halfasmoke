# key, room and memories

2021-Apr-29
1 minute read
eng

-

everyday she is granted a key to a room
she went in
one time she was so happy and didn’t want to leave
one time she couldn’t move even a finger upon entering
one time she was so frightened by all the pictures hanged on the wall
one time she just smiled and left
one time she didn’t enter any room, she sat down and looked, looked around, looked inward, inside of her and suddenly around her was filled with…
one time it was filled with flowers
one time she heard all favourite tunes
one time she saw her ‘selves’
and
who knows what key she will get tomorrow
but all this time, she was tired and longed for one key, THE big key, the key for the main door, the gate, the building, the palace
…

Was she too afraid to even try opening the gate by hands?
Was the gate even requiring a key to unlock?
What is out there for her, the outside world?
Was she just imagining things?
And most importantly, why is she there, in those rooms to begin with and were all of this nothing but memories, memories of a dream?

-R
