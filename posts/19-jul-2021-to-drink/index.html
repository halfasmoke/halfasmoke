# to drink

2021-Jul-19
0 minute read
eng

-

to drink is
to forget something
to remember something
to make something happen
to wish of undone something
to feel what word failed to say
and to see what action failed to do
drink once to this
drink twice to that
drink finite to the infinite

-R
