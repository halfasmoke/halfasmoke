# the hill

2021-Aug-29
eng

-

It all began on top of the hill, excited and fun, and slowly slided down until they reached the ‘valley of despair’ where all they can see is nothing but fog, mist, dark, and not everyone can get out of it, hence the name.
But those who keep rolling despite the heavy baggage, the string, pulling them back, those who have strength, courage will see the end of it, that is, a glimpse of hope in the form of a light beam shining down on their path.
Those will see the top of the hill again, another one where they can rejoice in what they called happiness but this time with a sense of appreciation because they now know the value of it.

“mọi chuyện dường như quá đỗi bình thường”
